# Nerdy Tutoring — Policy Knowledge Base

## Scheduling and Cancellation
Sessions can be rescheduled or cancelled up to 12 hours in advance at no charge.
Cancellations inside 12 hours are billed at half the session rate. Tutoring
sessions are booked in 60-minute blocks.

## Refunds and Satisfaction Guarantee
The first session is fully refundable if the family is not satisfied, requested
within 7 days. After the first session, unused prepaid hours are refundable on a
pro-rated basis. There is no long-term contract; plans are month-to-month.

## Tutor Matching
Families are matched to a tutor based on subject, grade level, and schedule
within 48 hours of enrollment. A family may request a different tutor at any
time at no cost.

## Subjects and Grade Levels
Tutoring is available for K-12 and college subjects including math, reading,
writing, science, and standardized test prep (SAT, ACT, state exams).

## Session Format and Tools
Sessions are delivered 1-on-1 over a live online classroom with a shared
whiteboard. Session recordings and progress notes are available to parents after
each session on the Standard and Intensive plans.

## Privacy and Recording
Calls and sessions may be recorded for quality and training. Student data is
handled under FERPA-aligned practices and is never sold.
