# Competitive Battlecards

## Versus Self-Service Apps
Self-service apps give practice problems but no live human accountability. Nerdy
pairs the student with a matched 1-on-1 tutor and sends progress notes to
parents, which keeps students accountable week to week.

## Versus Local In-Person Tutoring
Local tutoring is limited by who is nearby and by commute time. Nerdy matches
from a national tutor pool by subject, grade, and schedule within 48 hours, and
sessions run online so there is no travel.

## Versus Other Online Tutoring Services
Many services lock families into multi-month contracts. Nerdy is month-to-month
with a fully refundable first session and the ability to switch tutors at any
time at no cost.
